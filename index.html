OUR MOTTO

The goals set forth by the NCC in 1988 have remained relevant, effectively addressing the present socio-economic demands of the country.Develop a disciplined, organized, and motivated youth force equipped to provide leadership and dedicated service across all sectors of society.Inspire and prepare the youth for potential careers in the armed forces by fostering a conducive and supportive environment.Promote the development of key virtues such as character, comradeship, discipline, leadership, a secular outlook, a spirit of adventure, and a commitment to selfless service among young people across the nation.

OUR PLEDGE

We the cadets of the National Cadet Corps, do solemnly pledge that we shall always uphold the unity of India. We resolve to be disciplined and responsible citizens of our nation. We shall undertake positive community service in the spirit of selflessness and concern for our fellow beings.

Unity and Discipline | एकता और अनुशासन

The National Cadet Corps (NCC) teaches the values of unity and discipline to young people across the country. By participating in NCC, cadets learn to work together as a team, making friends from different backgrounds and cultures. They also learn the importance of discipline, such as being on time, taking responsibility, and respecting leaders. Through group activities, training, and community service, cadets see that working together makes them stronger. These lessons help cadets become responsible and dedicated citizens, ready to serve their country with honesty and hard work.

In 2023, NCC was started at IIT Hyderabad, providing a great opportunity for students to participate in NCC activities. The NCC at IIT Hyderabad allows students to develop leadership skills, physical fitness, and a sense of national pride. Through adventure activities like trekking, camping, and parades, cadets face challenges and overcome them together, building confidence and resilience. They learn to appreciate the value of perseverance and hard work, preparing them for future success in any field. NCC also fosters a sense of social responsibility, encouraging cadets to contribute positively to their communities. This initiative has been a significant addition to the campus, enhancing the holistic development of students and strengthening their commitment to the nation's unity and discipline.
